About AI Velocity Fund

Pioneering Market-Neutral
Intelligence.

AI Velocity Fund is a pioneering, market-neutral hedge fund that redefines investment through a commitment to consistent, low-volatility performance. We integrate state-of-the-art artificial intelligence with financial expertise, utilizing cutting-edge quantitative trading strategies. Our experienced team of portfolio managers are programmers who build, test, and execute proprietary algorithms designed to deliver reliable, non-volatile gains regardless of overall market conditions.

The AI advantage allows us to process massive data streams in real-time, uncover hidden opportunities, and adapt to shifting markets faster than ever. We emphasize smarter risk management, including predicting volatility and optimizing portfolio allocation, ensuring every investment decision is informed by both cutting-edge algorithms and seasoned market judgment.

Our Approach

Built on Three Pillars

01

Algorithmic Precision

Every trade is driven by proprietary algorithms built, tested, and refined by our team of programmer-portfolio managers. No guesswork — only systematic, data-driven execution.

02

Market Neutrality

Our strategies are designed to generate consistent returns regardless of broader market direction. We maintain a market-neutral posture through disciplined hedging and portfolio construction.

03

Intelligent Risk Management

AI-driven risk assessment continuously monitors volatility, optimizes portfolio allocation, and simulates thousands of scenarios to keep every position aligned with our performance objectives.

Meet the Team

Managing Partner

Dan Savage

Dan Savage serves as Managing Partner of AI Velocity Fund and Co-Chairman of MachineTrader.io Inc., the technology platform supporting the fund's trading infrastructure. A lifelong entrepreneur, Dan previously served as CEO of Predictive Technology Systems and co-founded Resolute Digital, a successful digital marketing agency.

He was an early pioneer in applying automation and data-driven systems to online advertising, including developing one of the first automated platforms for purchasing Google AdWords. Dan brings extensive experience in scaling technology-driven businesses and aligning product innovation with long-term strategy.

Partner & Chief Technology Officer

Ben Sanders

Ben Sanders is Partner and Chief Technology Officer of AI Velocity Fund and Co-Chairman of MachineTrader.io Inc., where he oversees the firm's technology platform and systems architecture. A lifelong computer scientist, Ben began programming at the age of seven.

His background includes senior technical leadership roles in Big Five advertising agencies, development of online banking systems for Chase on Wall Street, and co-founding Resolute Digital as Chief Technology Officer. Ben leads the design and execution of the fund's trading infrastructure and algorithmic systems.

Partner & Chief Investment Officer

Jerzy Pawlowski, Ph.D.

Jerzy Pawlowski serves as Partner and Chief Investment Officer of AI Velocity Fund, bringing deep expertise in portfolio management, quantitative finance, and risk modeling. His background spans credit derivatives, emerging markets, and structured products including CDOs.

He holds a Ph.D. in Physics and teaches graduate-level courses in R for Finance and Algorithmic Portfolio Management at New York University. Jerzy oversees investment strategy, portfolio construction, and risk management across all fund strategies.

Chief Financial Officer

John "Jack" Marhofer

John Marhofer serves as Chief Financial Officer of AI Velocity Fund, providing financial leadership to support the firm's growth and operational discipline. Previously, Jack was CFO of Resolute Digital and played a key role in its successful exit.

He also served as CFO of Cleveland BioLabs, Inc., where he helped complete an IPO and raise over $100 million in funding. Jack brings extensive experience in financial strategy, capital markets, and corporate governance.

Marketing Director

Caroline Yakar

Caroline Yakar is the Marketing Director at AI Velocity Fund, overseeing marketing operations, communications, and the firm's digital presence.

Caroline supported the launch and maintains platform growth of MachineTrader.io through client support, marketing, web management, and analytics. Earlier in her career, Caroline was a Senior Underwriter at Sun Life Financial, and later co-owned a tourism business in Turkey with her husband.
